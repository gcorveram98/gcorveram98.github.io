Our Mission

Voices Without Barriers Foundation is a Non-Profit Organization where our main focus is assisting our community with any sort of translation services which includes:

  • Translation for Immigration Appointments at the U.S. Citizenship & Immigration Services (USCIS),
  • Court hearings,
  • Interpretation,
  • Language Classes,
  • Public Assistance Hearings.

While Voices Without Barriers Foundation holds their focus on interviews and Government assistance, they also hold many resources for those who would love to learn any language that they offer such as: Spanish, German, Mandarin & Cantonese, Korean, and Japanese. The community is welcome to inquire about the services provided and language courses that are offered. Free of charge.
